About Me

Business Intelligence & Data Analytics Student | Strategic Thinker | Sports Enthusiast

Charity Mese - BIDA Student

Hello, I'm Charity Mese

I'm a passionate and driven 20-year-old student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Business Intelligence and Data Analytics at Botswana Accountancy College. I believe that data holds the key to solving complex problems and driving innovation in today's digital world.

My Background

Growing up in Botswana, I developed a keen interest in technology and problem-solving from an early age. This led me to pursue my current degree, where I'm learning to transform raw data into meaningful insights that can inform strategic business decisions. My academic journey has equipped me with skills in data analysis, visualization, and statistical modeling.

  • Football: As both a player and spectator, I love the teamwork, strategy, and excitement of football. I play regularly with friends and follow both local and international leagues.
